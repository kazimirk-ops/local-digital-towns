Buy & Sell
A local marketplace built for real people. Buy and sell with cash, card, Venmo, or PayPal. Hold items with deposits. Offer local delivery or ship statewide through our Florida Network.
Buyers: Browse listings, message sellers directly, and pay the way you prefer. Filter by category, price, or condition.

Sellers: Create a listing with photos, set your price and payment methods, and manage orders from your dashboard.

Community Pulse
Real-time community data: live fishing conditions from NOAA (tides, water temp, wind, daily fishing score), safety grade with local resources and emergency contacts, and area news.
Open Pulse to see today's fishing conditions pulled from NOAA's St. Lucie Inlet station. Scroll down for the Safety section with current grade, emergency numbers, and local resources. Data refreshes automatically.

Groups & Events
Join discussion groups for locals — share news, photos, fishing reports, and neighborhood updates. Browse community events or create your own. Verified locals get access to private groups.
Members: Join groups, post messages, react to posts, and share images. Use the search to find groups by topic.

Moderators: Create a group, approve members, and manage posts from the mod dashboard. Private groups require approval to join.

Customers: Browse available gigs, view provider profiles, and book directly. Message providers with questions before booking.

Providers: List your services, set your rates, and manage bookings from your dashboard.

OUR MISSION

Social Media the Way
It Was Meant to Be

Before the algorithms, before the ads, before the bots — social media was just people talking to people. We're building that back.

🤖 What Happened to the Internet

The platforms that were supposed to connect us have been quietly replaced by AI-generated content, bot accounts, paid promotions, and algorithmic feeds designed to keep you scrolling — not connecting.

When you don't know if the person you're talking to is real, trust breaks down. When every post is competing for your attention, community breaks down. We're fixing that — locally.

🌊 The FloridaLife Way

✅ Every account is a real person
✅ No AI chatbots in conversations
✅ No algorithmic feed — newest first, always
✅ No ads, no promoted posts, no sponsorships
✅ No data sold to anyone, ever
✅ Locals get verified, locals get more features
✅ Commerce stays local — your money stays in your community

🛠️ AI as a Tool, Not a Weapon

We believe AI is one of the most powerful tools ever built — and like any powerful tool, what matters is who's holding it and why.

AI should help you find a fishing spot, price your item fairly, or draft a message. It should not be pretending to be your neighbor, manipulating what you see, or harvesting your behavior to sell you things you don't need.

"AI should be a tool that empowers people — not something deployed on them without their knowledge or consent."
