Delete Your Account

To request deletion of your Sebastian Florida account and all associated data, please email us at:

support@sebastian-florida.com

Include the email address associated with your account in your request.

What Gets Deleted

Upon processing your request, we will permanently delete: your account profile, email address, display name, order history, delivery addresses, and any content you have posted.

Timeline

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email when your data has been deleted.

Data Retained

We may retain anonymized transaction records as required for tax and legal compliance. No personally identifiable information is kept after deletion.
